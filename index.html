Trivia Game - The simpsons

A trivia game that shows only one question until the player answers it or their time runs out. If the player selects the correct answer, show a screen congratulating them for choosing the right option. After a few seconds, display the next question. The scenario is similar for wrong answers and time-outs. If the player runs out of time, tell the player that time's up and display the correct answer. If the player chooses the wrong answer, tell the player they selected the wrong option and then display the correct answer. Wait a few seconds, then show the next question. On the final screen, show the number of correct answers, incorrect answers, and an option to restart the game (without reloading the page).
